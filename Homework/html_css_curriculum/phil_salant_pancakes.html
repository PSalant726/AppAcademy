Buttermilk Pancakes

    Description Ingredients Directions

Description

Light and fluffy, these are classic buttermilk pancakes !

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/8 cups buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Directions

  1. Preheat and lightly grease a large skillet
  2. Mix the flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl
  3. Add the egg, buttermilk, and butter; stir together lightly
  4. The batter should look thick, spongy, and puffy
  5. Drop 1/3 cup of the batter onto the cooking surface, spreading with the bottom of the cup
  6. Cook until lightly-browned on each side, 1 to 2 minutes per
